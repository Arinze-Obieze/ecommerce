Product Creation And Seller Product Management

  • Reworked the seller product creation flow into a cleaner publish-oriented sequence covering variants, media, specifications, compliance, SKU handling, and final review.
  • Added structured readiness checks and step completion rules so sellers can clearly see what is still blocking submission.
  • Expanded wizard state handling for specification summaries, structured specification entries, and safer draft restoration.
  • Introduced a seller product detail experience for reviewing and editing product data, media, pricing, stock, specifications, and bulk discount tiers.
  • Upgraded the seller products dashboard with richer list views and bulk product actions such as duplicate, archive, unarchive, and delete.
  • Extended store product APIs to support richer detail reads, editing, duplication, slug generation, SKU generation, draft persistence, and moderation-aware product handling.

Inventory Operations

  • Added a dedicated store inventory API for reads, direct stock adjustments, variant adjustments, and low-stock restocking actions.
  • Added inventory summaries for total products, low-stock items, out-of-stock items, and variant-managed products.
  • Logged inventory activity with adjustment metadata, reasons, notes, actor context, and history retrieval for operational traceability.
  • Rebuilt the seller inventory dashboard with search, filtering, manual stock controls, quick restock flows, variant inspection, and recent adjustment history.

Product Detail, Cart, And Mobile Shopping Experience

  • Redesigned the product detail page with richer mixed-media support for both images and videos.
  • Improved product storytelling through overview content, specifications, return-policy presentation, and mobile-friendly collapsible sections.
  • Improved mobile buying flow with a sticky purchase CTA and clearer selected-variant feedback.
  • Strengthened cart and product-card behavior to preserve selected size and color context through shopping and add-to-cart interactions.
  • Refined cart quantity controls and responsive layout so product adjustments are easier on smaller screens.
  • Improved mobile quick-view layering, CTA stability, image sizing, and immersive fullscreen image inspection.

Checkout, Delivery, Orders, And Fulfillment

  • Improved add-to-cart and post-payment feedback behavior by removing duplicate success messaging and strengthening payment-success routing into order review.
  • Added a dedicated customer order-details experience showing metadata, payment reference, status information, product lines, quantities, totals, and variant details.
  • Connected recent activity and order history entries directly to individual order-detail pages.
  • Added clearer order-progress presentation based on status, fulfillment state, escrow state, and lifecycle markers.
  • Enforced delivery-address capture before payment and added order-level shipping-address snapshot support.
  • Reworked checkout into a clearer step-based flow with cart review first and delivery details collected in a dedicated second-step modal.
  • Improved Nigeria-only delivery input quality with searchable state/city selection and fallback typed city support.
  • Added seller-side order detail and fulfillment controls, including tracking-reference capture and internal note history.

Account, Navigation, And Profile Improvements
